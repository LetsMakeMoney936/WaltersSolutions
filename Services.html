Our Services

Roof Repair & Maintenance

Ensuring your roof is in top condition is essential to prevent leaks and water damage. Services include patching, resealing, and replacing damaged roofing materials.

Plumbing Repair

Repairs to the water system, including pipes, tanks, faucets, water heaters, and toilets. Common issues include leaks, clogged drains, or faulty water pumps.

Electrical System Repairs

Troubleshooting and fixing issues with the electrical system, such as power outlets, batteries, fuses, or wiring, ensuring everything is safely functional.

AC & Heating Repairs

Repairing or replacing air conditioning units and heaters to ensure proper temperature control. This service also includes maintenance on the ventilation system.

Refrigerator & Appliance Repairs

Repairing or replacing malfunctioning appliances like refrigerators, stoves, and microwaves.

Chassis Repair

Repair an issue with the chassis like bent frame, broken axle hangers, crossmembers, outriggers, A-Frame or Pin box.

Slide-Out Repair

Fixing issues with slide-outs, which can include malfunctioning motors, seals, or track misalignment.

Water Heater & Furnace Repair

Repairing or replacing malfunctioning water heaters and furnaces to keep you comfortable all year long. This includes fixing issues with gas or electric systems.

Seal & Window Replacement

Repairing or replacing window seals and glass to prevent leaks and maintain insulation. This also includes re-sealing doors and windows to improve energy efficiency.

Generator Repair

Maintaining and repairing generators, ensuring that the power supply remains reliable when you're off the grid. Services include oil changes, filter replacements, and engine repairs.

Exterior Body & Paint Repair

Fixing dents, scratches, and other cosmetic damage to the exterior.

Awning Repair & Replacement

Repairing or replacing broken or damaged awnings, including fabric or motor replacements. This service ensures your awning is functioning properly for shade and shelter.

Flooring & Interior Repair

Services to repair or replace damaged flooring, upholstery, cabinets, and other interior components. This service includes restoring damaged walls or structures in the living area.

Battery & Charging System Service

Ensuring that the batteries are functioning properly, including replacing old batteries and repairing the charging system to maintain power for off-grid living.

Tow System & Hitch Repair

Repairing or installing towing systems and hitches to ensure safe towing. This includes replacing hitches, brakes, and wiring for trailers.

Vent & Exhaust Repair

Repairing the ventilation system, including fixing exhaust fans, vent covers, and ensuring proper airflow throughout the vehicle.
